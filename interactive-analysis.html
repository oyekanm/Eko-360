This section captures interactive dashboard of different sectors. Click the dropdown button to view analytics across health, education and economic affairs.

School Infrastructures and Library

This interactive dashboard lets you explore the usability and state of public libraries and classrooms in public schools. It covers the rate and distribution of library patronage in public libraries, distribution and percentage of unused classrooms and classrooms under construction(for both primary and secondary schools) in each LGA, and percentage of unused classrooms and computers in GTCs. The dataset covers the period from 2009/10 to 2018/19.

Awards and Scholarships in Lagos State Tertiary Institutions

This interactive dashboard lets you explore number and distribution of students who receive awards and scholarships in Lagos State tertiary institutions. The scholarships are categorised by local and foreign scholarships while awards are issued by the state bursary. The distribution is done according to the five divisions of Lagos State. The dataset covers the period from 2009/10 to 2018/19.

Lagos State Electricity Consumption

This interactive economic affairs dashboard lets you explore data patterns and trends of various projects by the Lagos State Electricity Board across each Local Government Area (LGA). It covers electricity consumption in MW, capacity installed by independent power plants in Lagos, and energy consumption under independent power plants. The dataset covers the period 2010 to 2021. Click on a map area to get detailed data on any particular Local Government Area (LGA).

Lagos State Commerce and Agricultural Statistics

This interactive economic affairs dashboard lets you explore data patterns and trends of commerce and agricultural statistics across each Local Government Area (LGA) in Lagos. It covers the number and types of animals slaughtered, the number of approved markets, and approved slaughter slabs and abattoirs. The dataset covers the period 2010 to 2021. Click on a map area to get detailed data on any particular Local Government Area (LGA).

Lagos State Works and Infrastructure Statistics

Accident Statistics

This interactive economic affairs dashboard lets you explore data patterns and trends of accident statistics in Lagos. It covers road and rail accidents.
